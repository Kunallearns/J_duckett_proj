Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted, add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate untill served
